# Introduction
I am a huge fan of VScode for coding. This has become my goto editor for any programing applications like python, html, markdown, css, Javascript etc. If you have read *About* section of this blog you would have noticed i am a electronics engineer with masters in embedded system. Thus standard Micro controllers and Micro processors are not new to me. But they are like old friends who never leave your side. This is the Post Corona period, i had opened my tinkering box to find couple of arduino nano laying around, I wanted to play with them for old time sake and installed arduino IDE and started to work on arduino IDE. As by now i was accustomed to using VScode my senses were saying can we use VScode to program arduino, in fact i knew that Atmel studio is build around visual studio platform. Thus thought let me check what is around on the internet these days. Voilà!! There it was PlatformIO. Although i have arrived at this party late, I thought let me put my experience on my blog which can at least serve as a quick catchup guide for me in future.
